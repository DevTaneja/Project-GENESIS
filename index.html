GENESIS

Grades Evaluation Network with Enhanced Simulation and Intelligence System


GENESIS Features

Calculator

The logic behind the CGPA calculator involves converting grades into grade points based on predefined ranges. These grade points are then multiplied by the corresponding number of credits assigned to each subject. The total grade points are divided by the total credit points available and then multiplied by 10 to obtain the CGPA.

Analyser

Analyser, a pioneering tool, determines exact Endsem exam marks required for achieving desired CGPA, utilizing existing Insem scores. Users input Insem results and target CGPA to unveil calculated Endsem requisites, enhancing precision in academic goal-setting.

Simulator

The Simulator inputs desired CGPA, simulates mark scenarios, and suggests mark variations for achieving it, specifying required marks for Insem and Endsem exams per subject. Users can allocate marks based on preference or existing scores. The algorithm, derived from perfect 10 CGPA achievers, calculates minimum marks needed per subject, considering difficulty level.

Aim Assistor

The Aim Assistor tool lets users input their target CGPA and customize subject difficulty levels with sliders. It calculates required Insem and Endsem marks based on user-set difficulty, offering tailored insights into academic requirements for goal achievement.
