GENESIS

Grades Evaluation Network with Enhanced Simulation and Inmtelligence System

Calculator

The basic logic behind the CGPA calculator involves converting grades into grade points based on predefined ranges. These grade points are then multiplied by the corresponding number of credits assigned to each subject. The total grade points are divided by the total credit points available and then multiplied by 10 to obtain the CGPA.

Aim Assistor

Input desired CGPA, customize subject difficulty with sliders. Receive tailored insights on required Insem and Endsem marks for goal-oriented academic planning, empowering users to make informed decisions based on their individual preferences and academic goals.

Analyser

Analyser determines Endsem marks needed for desired CGPA based on existing Insem marks. Input Insem marks and target CGPA to calculate Endsem targets, aiding effective goal setting.

Simulator

The tool generates subject mark scenarios to achieve desired CGPA, determining feasible mark variations, including Insem and Endsem targets. Users allocate marks, with algorithm based on perfect CGPA achievers' data, adjusting for subject difficulty.
